Before you start, check you GMail security settings to make sure the application will be able to access your account. Instructions can be found at Setting up your GMail Account

Step 1. Enter your GMail Account Information and download up to the last 2000 emails to your local VM
(All of the emails are only stored locally to your VM)

Step 2. Select an email address to ingest and analyze

Step 3. Once the ingest has completed successfully click here to analyze the results.

The download and ingest process may take up to 30 minutes to complete. You will receive an alert that the process has completed. In the mean time you may check out the features and an introduction video of the application.
